Crockpot Poached Salmon | Dinner

4 serving10min / 3hours

Such a yummy salmon flavor!

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon steaks or 2 fillets
  • 4 ounces water
  • 2 ounces beer
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 peppercorns
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 teaspoon onion flakes

Directions

  1. Lightly grease bottom of crockpot and put fish in.
  2. Mix all other ingredients and bring to a boil in a saucepan on the stove-top
  3. Pour mixture over fish
  4. Cook on low for 3 hours
  5. Serve with rice
