Chicken ST Mix | Dinner

1 serving5min / 15min

Quick spiced chicken bits.

Ingredients

  • 1 boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup basmati rice
  • 3/4 cup water

Spices to taste

  • TexJoy Steak Seasoning
  • Fiesta(brand) Extra Fancty Texas Style Steak Seasoning
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Balsamic Vinegar
  • Ground Coriander
  • Minced Garlic
  • Orange Peel

Directions

  1. Start cooking basmati rice in 3/4 cup water.
  2. Slice chicken into bit size pieces
  3. Season chicken to taste, starting with the steak seasonings. Use only a smallamount of balsamic vinegar and orange peel.
  4. Grill chicken in a george forman until cooked through.
  5. Eat and NJoy
