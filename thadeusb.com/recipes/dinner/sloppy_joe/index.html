Sloppy Joe | Dinner

Yum

Ingredients

  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
  • 1 can (8 oz) tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Generous amount of Applewood Seasoning ( or similar steak season )
  • Tiny amount of Cayenne Pepper
  • Tiny amount of seasoned salt
  • Onion Powder
  • Peppercorn & Garlic
  • Buns
  • Ground Beef

Directions

  1. Cook ground beef w/ all seasonings, chop as fine as possible with spatula while cooking
  2. In mixing bowl, combine tomato paste, sauce, and water
  3. When beef is cooked and slightly blackened, add the tomato combo
  4. Place on bun and enjoy
