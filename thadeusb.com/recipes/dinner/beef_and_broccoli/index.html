Beef And Broccoli | Dinner

4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 pound flank steak, sliced into thin strips
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ teaspoon ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • ⅓ cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • ¼ cup honey
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 4 cups broccoli florets
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • Sesame seeds, optional

Directions

  1. In oiled skillet sear steak. Remove from pan.
  2. Add garlic and ginger and saute.
  3. Combine sesame oil, brown sugar, honey, and beef broth and broccoli and stir.
  4. Combine cornstarch and water then add to broccoli mixture.
  5. Bring to a boil, until sauce thickens.
  6. Pour over steak.
  7. Serve over rice.
